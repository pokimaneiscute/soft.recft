About SoFT

Welcome to the Society of Food Technologists (SoFT), a club dedicated to fostering a passion for food technology within our department!

Our mission is to provide a platform where students can explore, learn and excel in the field of food technology. Through various activities, workshopd and events, we aim to enhance your knowledge, skills and networking capabilities.

At SoFT, our goals are twofold: to educate and connect. We strive to educate our members about the latest advancements in food technology, industry trends and innovative techniques. By organizing workshops, lectures and seminars led by professionals in the field, we ensure that our members are well-informed and equipped with the necessary knowledge.

Additionally, we prioritize creating connections within the industry. Through guest lectures, industry visits and networking events, we expose our members to potential employers, mentors and peers who share their passion for food technology. These connections can be invaluable for internships, job opportunities and career growth.

By joining and actively participating in SoFT's activities, members can enjoy numerous benefits. Some of these include:

1. Skill Development:

Enhance and refine your technical and practical skills through hands-on workshops, lab sessions and industry projects.

2. Knowledge Expansion:

Stay up-to-date with the latest advancements, emerging technologies and research in the field of food technology through expert-led presentations and educational sessions.

3. Networking Opportunities:

Connect with professionals, industry experts and like-minded individuals in the field during guest lectures, conferences and networking events.

4. Personal Growth:

Develop leadership, teamwork and organizational skills by taking part in managing and organizing club activities and events.
